Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit anonymous feedback? +

Simply leave the name field blank when submitting your feedback. Your privacy is completely protected.

How can I track my feedback status? +

Visit our Status page and use your submission ID to track the progress of your feedback in real-time.

What types of issues can I report? +

You can report general feedback, maintenance issues, suggestions, complaints, or any concerns about our services.

How long does it take to get a response? +

On average, we respond within 10 hours. Urgent maintenance issues are prioritized and addressed within 2-4 hours.
